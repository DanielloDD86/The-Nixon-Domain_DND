Divine Domain - Nixon

Example Deity - Richard Nixon

Nixon Spells:

1st - Command, Disguise Self
3rd - Suggestion, Mirror Image
5th - Blindness/Deafness, Sleet Storm
7th - Confusion, Wall of Fire
9th - Cloudkill, Dominate Person

Feature:
1st - Profinciency - Intimidation, Heavy Armour
    - "Smoke-Filled Room" - 3/long rest - Using a shield, you can create a 5-foot radius of smoke, anyone in that 
      radius is stunned and you become blinded. - Dex Save - Cleric Spell Save DC

2nd - Channel Divinity - Private Healthcare

6th - Starting at 6th level, you have to pick a designated player from your party.
      Afterwards you will be able to heal this player outside of your own range.

8th - Divine Strike -  At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with poison â€“ a gift from your deity.
      Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 fire damage to the target.

17th - 1/long rest - At 17th level, you are able to summon and command a "Douglas C-47 Skytrain" into any 20-foot radius,
       creating a giant explosion dealing 4d6 fire damage on a successful save, and 8d6 on a failed save.
       (yes, you do just get one fireball every long rest :] )

Channel Divinity:
- By expending a use of your Channel Divinity, you are able to heal one of your allies using the power of your deities healthcare,
  proportional to the "donation" you give. (example 1d10 of healing being five gold) - the amount of dice you can get from
  donation is also proportional to half your cleric level (rounded down).
  The number of times you can use this feature is based on the level you've reached in this class:
  2nd level, once; 6th level, twice; and 18th level, thrice. You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
